Brand · Logo system

SyncMaster — official lockups.

SVG · PNG@3× · Self-contained

01 / Primary lockup

Horizontal — purple wordmark

The primary horizontal lockup leads with the acid disc and a brand-purple SYNCMASTER wordmark. Default everywhere there's a light or paper-toned surface — homepage hero, deck title slides, marketing collateral, email headers.

Primary · on paper

02 / Horizontal variants

Purple for light · white for dark

Two variants — same disc, swap the wordmark colour to match the surface. Purple wordmark on any light surface (paper, muted, secondary). White wordmark on ink, ink-raised, or purple surfaces.

Light surface — purple wordmark
Dark surface — white wordmark

03 / Stacked lockup

When vertical space is what you've got

Drop to the stacked form for square avatars, app-store tiles, side panels, business cards — anywhere the horizontal form would crop awkwardly. Same two-variant rule: purple on light, white on dark.

Stacked · light surface
Stacked · dark surface

04 / Mark only

Disc + glyph, no wordmark

The mark works on every surface unchanged — the acid disc carries the contrast, and the purple glyph reads on light, dark, and brand-purple alike. Use for favicons, app icons, the dashboard sidebar mark, footer chips, and anywhere a small monogram is needed.

On paper
On ink
On purple

05 / Scale

Mark holds at any size

Single filled vector shape with a thin lime knockout at the disc edge — crisp from 24 px favicon up to billboard.

24 px · favicon
48 px · footer
96 px · app icon
160 px · marketing
240 px · hero

06 / Colors

Two colors. That's it.

Official SVGs freeze acid lime as #C9E84C — the canonical logo hex. Use #C9E84C anywhere the logo is rasterised or imported into a vector editor; use the --sm-acid CSS token anywhere it lives inside HTML/CSS.

Acid lime — disc --sm-acid · logo hex #C9E84C
Brand purple — glyph & wordmark --sm-purple #4B4BC0
Paper — dark-mode wordmark --sm-paper #FFFFFF
