# PROJECT: SyncMaster

## Overview
SyncMaster is a curated sync licensing platform designed to connect independent composers and artists with film, TV, advertising, gaming, and brand opportunities through structured sync briefs and streamlined rights workflows.

The platform focuses on:
- Quality over volume
- Curated composer network
- Structured sync briefs
- Fast licensing workflow
- Rights clarity and administration
- Global + emerging market focus (especially Africa)

---

# Core Positioning

SyncMaster is a curated sync brief marketplace that helps producers find the right music faster and helps composers access real opportunities.
