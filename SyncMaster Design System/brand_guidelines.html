03 · Identity

Logo Usage

Three lockups · Three rules

CLEAR SPACE · DO

  • ✓ Maintain clear space equal to the height of the mark on all sides.
  • ✓ Use the acid-on-ink lockup on every dark surface.
  • ✓ Use the purple wordmark only on pure white.
  • ✓ Place the mark at full opacity. No tints, no overlays.

DON'T

  • ✗ Don't recolour the acid lime or the purple.
  • ✗ Don't stretch, rotate, or warp the mark.
  • ✗ Don't place the mark on photography without a 60% scrim.
  • ✗ Don't recreate letterforms — always use the artwork file.

06 · Voice

Tone of Voice

Five principles · From the brand context
PRINCIPLE 01

Direct and practical.

No marketing hedging. Lead with the verb. Cut the qualifier.

PRINCIPLE 02

Specific over vague.

3–5 matches, not 500. 24–48 hours, not "fast". Pair every claim with a number.

PRINCIPLE 03

Dark editorial.

Reads like a developer-tool product page about music. Operator's voice, not copywriter's.

PRINCIPLE 04

Building in public.

Show the data. Show the process. Show the roadmap. Confident without bravado.

PRINCIPLE 05

Never defensive.

Never defensive about African music. State the work. Let the catalogue speak.

DO · SAY THIS

  • → "3–5 curated matches — not 500 unvetted submissions."
  • → "No placement = no fee."
  • → "Apply once. Lifetime access."
  • → "Curated, cleared, and ready."

DON'T · AVOID

  • × Revolutionise · unleash · transform · powerful · seamless.
  • × Exclamation points. We never shout.
  • × Emoji in product or marketing copy.
  • × Apologies or qualifiers around "African".

07 · Imagery

Imagery Direction

Two modes · Photography · Editorial layout
CRITERIA · COLOUR

Warm shadows or neutral cool. Never desaturated grey. Skin tones rendered honestly — no orange filter, no instagram lift.

CRITERIA · GRAIN

Subtle film grain at 5–8%. Adds editorial weight. Never digital noise. Never sharpened past the camera default.

CRITERIA · CROP

Tight crops with room above the head for type overlay. 4:5 portrait or 16:9 landscape. No square crops outside social media.
