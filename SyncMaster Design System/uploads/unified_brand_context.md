# SyncMaster Unified Brand & Design Context

## Brand Strategy
**Mission:** "Connect African composers to global screens. Curated, cleared, and ready."
**Category:** Sync licensing infrastructure / African music placement agency.
**Model:** 15–20% commission on placement fees, charged to the producer. No placement = no fee.
**Tone of Voice:** Dark editorial. Confident without bravado. Direct and practical. Specific over vague. Building in public. Never defensive about African music.

## Personas
1. **Tunde (African Composer):** Primary. Pain: Rights complexity, metadata gaps, no access to briefs. Goal: Consistent income stream.
2. **Amara (Music Supervisor):** Secondary. Pain: Finding rights-clean African music fast. Goal: Reduce turnaround from brief to track.
3. **Nollywood (Validation):** Tertiary. Early validation.

## Design System Tokens
**Typography:**
- Primary (Sans): DM Sans
- Monospace: Geist Mono
- Headings: `font-weight: 900` (black), `letter-spacing: -0.068em`, `line-height: 1.2`
- Body: `font-weight: 500`

**Color Palette:**
- Background: `#ffffff` (Light) / `#0f0f1a` (Dark)
- Foreground (Text): `#111111` (Light) / `#f9f9f9` (Dark)
- Primary Brand: `#4b4bc0` (Syncmaster Purple)
- Active Badge Accent: `oklch(0.88 0.18 120)` (Acid Lime)
- Status Colors: Green `#22c55e`, Amber `#f59e0b`, Blue `#3b82f6`, Destructive `#ef4444`

**Radii & Layout:**
- Base Radius: `0.375rem` (6px)
- Base Spacing: `4px` unit
