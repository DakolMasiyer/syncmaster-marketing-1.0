# Advanced sync licensing: how to negotiate your first deal

## Hook

Advanced Sync Licensing: How to Negotiate Your First Deal

## Body

You have a placement offer on the table.

Most composers in this position accept the first number and sign quickly — partly from excitement, partly from fear of losing the deal. Both reactions are understandable. Both are expensive.

Here's what the most commercially savvy composers do when an offer arrives: they pause, check the five negotiable variables, and respond within 24 hours with a clear, professional counter or acceptance.

The five variables: upfront fee, exclusivity period, territory, term length, performance royalty setup.

None of these require a lawyer on the first conversation. They require knowing what's standard, what's movable, and what you're worth.
