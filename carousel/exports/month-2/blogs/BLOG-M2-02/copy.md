# Month 2: briefs, pitches, and placements — the honest update

## Hook

Month 2: Briefs, Pitches, and Placements — The Honest Update

## Body

Two months in. Here's what's actually happening inside SyncMaster.

Brief volume has doubled from Month 1. Placement rate is holding. Turnaround time is improving. The infrastructure is compounding.

But the numbers aren't the most interesting part of Month 2. The pattern is.

Every deal we've won has been won on speed and rights confidence. Every deal we've lost has been lost on response time — not music quality, not rights issues. Time.

The 48-hour brief window is real. Supervisors told us this in Month 1. Month 2 is confirming it with data.

The lesson for composers: your infrastructure has to be ready before the brief arrives. You cannot build it during the window.
