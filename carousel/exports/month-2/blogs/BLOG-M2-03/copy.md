# The African composer's playbook for international sync

## Hook

The African Composer's Playbook for International Sync

## Body

In Month 2, we received briefs from a European documentary, a global NGO campaign, and a North American streaming drama.

None of these were reached through direct outreach. They came through content — from the transparency of documenting what we're building here and on social.

But what made us able to respond to them was built before the briefs arrived: a vetted catalogue, cleared rights, tagged metadata, organised stems.

This is the playbook for African composers who want to reach international sync markets — not in theory, but in the next 90 days.

The practical steps: rights documentation, file preparation, metadata standards, PRO registration, and building a response infrastructure that works at brief speed.
