# Composer audit walkthrough — is your catalogue sync-ready?

## Hook

Is your catalogue actually sync-ready?

## Body

Most composers think the answer is yes. After two months of vetting applications at SyncMaster, we've found the honest answer is almost always: not yet — but fixable.

In this video, we walk through the 6-point composer audit we run on every SyncMaster application, using a real example from Month 2.

What we audit:
→ Rights chain documentation (master + publishing: confirmed and provable)
→ Sample clearance (zero tolerance — one uncleared sample stops the whole catalogue)
→ File quality (broadcast-spec WAV — not MP3)
→ Stems organisation (available, named, deliverable in under 2 hours)
→ Metadata completeness (ISRC, BPM, key, mood tags — all embedded)
→ Response infrastructure (sub-24-hour response — demonstrated, not theoretical)

We also walk through what failure at each point looks like — and why most of these gaps are fixable in under 4 weeks.
