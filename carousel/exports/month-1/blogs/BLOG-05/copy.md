# Month 1: What We Learned About the Sync Gap

## Hook

Month 1: What We Learned About the Sync Gap

## Body

One month of building SyncMaster. Here's the honest retrospective.

6 composers vetted. 3 briefs received. 1 placement confirmed. 20 applications reviewed. 14 composers given specific written feedback on what to fix.

But the numbers aren't the story. The patterns are.

The infrastructure gap is both wider and more fixable than we expected. The demand from music supervisors is more explicit than anticipated. And the talent — the actual music being made by African composers — is ready for the global market right now.
