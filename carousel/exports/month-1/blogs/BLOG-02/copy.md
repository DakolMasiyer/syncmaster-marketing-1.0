# From Brief to Delivery in 48 Hours: Inside the Curation Process

## Hook

From Brief to Delivery in 48 Hours: Inside the SyncMaster Curation Process

## Body

A production company sent us a brief on Monday morning. By Wednesday afternoon, they had 5 curated, cleared, brief-matched tracks from African composers.

Here's exactly how that happened — and why the speed mattered more than anything else.

The brief specified: 60–90 seconds, Afrofusion with organic elements, cinematic and building, no vocals, one-stop clearance required, delivery in 48 hours.

We didn't scramble. We searched. Because the catalogue was already built.

The 48-hour delivery was only possible because the work happened before the brief arrived — composers vetted in advance, tracks tagged by mood and instrumentation, rights confirmed for every track in the system.
