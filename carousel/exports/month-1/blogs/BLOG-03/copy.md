# Why African Music Belongs on Global Screens

## Hook

Why African Music Belongs on Global Screens — And Why the System Isn't Built for It Yet

## Body

The data is unambiguous: African music has achieved global reach.

Burna Boy headlining international festivals. Tems winning global awards. Amapiano appearing in South Korean dramas and British luxury campaigns. Wizkid on playlists in 180 countries.

The music is there. The demand is real. The audience is global.

So why are African composers still fighting for access to sync licensing — the most lucrative placement market in the music industry?

This article looks at the demand side, the infrastructure gap, and what's being built to close it.
