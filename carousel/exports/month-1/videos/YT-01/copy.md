# Why we\

## Hook

Most sync platforms weren't built with African composers in mind.

## Body

The major sync libraries serve the global creator market brilliantly. But they weren't designed for the specific challenges facing composers from Lagos, Nairobi, Accra, Johannesburg, or Kigali.

The rights structures are different. The access to supervisors is different. The metadata infrastructure doesn't exist. The licensing knowledge gap is wide.

In this video, we explain exactly why we're building SyncMaster:
→ The $500M global sync market and Africa's missing share
→ What we found when we started talking to African composers
→ Why infrastructure, not talent, is the missing piece
→ What we're building and how it works
