# 48-hour curation turnaround story

## Hook

A production company sent us a brief on Monday morning.

## Body

By Wednesday afternoon, they had 5 curated tracks. All cleared. All brief-matched. All from African composers.

I want to talk about what that turnaround actually required — because it wasn't fast because we rushed. It was fast because we'd done the work in advance.

The 48-hour delivery was only possible because of what happened in the weeks before the brief arrived:

→ Composers vetted (rights confirmed, files in spec, metadata tagged)
→ Catalogue indexed by mood, tempo, instrumentation
→ A team trained to read briefs and match precisely, not broadly

Most sync intermediaries take a week or more to respond to a brief. By then, the production has moved on.

Speed is trust in this industry. When a supervisor gets 5 strong options in 48 hours, they send you the next brief too.

Building that trust one brief at a time.
