# 90 Days Building SyncMaster: The Honest Numbers

## Hook

[MONTH 3 BLOG — 90 Days Building SyncMaster: The Honest Numbers]

## Body

→ Publish at syncmaster.io/blog

Open with the exact numbers, no rounding:

47 applications. 12 composers. 9 briefs. 31 tracks submitted. 3 confirmed placements.

Cover each metric with honesty:
— Applications: what we expected vs. what arrived. Where they came from. Who wasn't ready.
— Vetting: the real rejection reasons — rights not confirmed, metadata gaps, unreachable co-writers.
— Briefs: who sent them, what categories, what they paid.
— Submissions: what got pitched vs. what was selected. Why.
— Placements: the three confirmed stories, specific enough to be real.

Middle section: what we got wrong in Month 1 that we fixed in Month 2.
End section: what Month 3 is designed to prove — brief volume, simultaneous pitches, scaling to 20 composers.

Tone: completely transparent. No PR language. Actual numbers and honest failures.
Length: 1,200–1,500 words.
